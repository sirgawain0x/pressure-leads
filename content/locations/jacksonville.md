## Why Jacksonville Properties Need Regular Pressure Washing

Jacksonville sits at the convergence of the St. Johns River, the Intracoastal Waterway, and the Atlantic Ocean. This creates a microclimate that's even more challenging for property maintenance than inland Florida locations:

- **Higher humidity levels** from surrounding waterways feed mold and algae growth year-round
- **Salt air exposure** extends well inland from the coast, accelerating surface deterioration
- **Heavy live oak canopy** across established neighborhoods drops debris, pollen, and tannins that stain surfaces
- **Afternoon thunderstorms** from May through October keep surfaces consistently damp
- **Sandy soil** means more dust and particulate matter settling on exterior surfaces

Most Jacksonville homes benefit from annual house washing, annual driveway cleaning, and roof soft washing every 2–3 years. Properties near water or under heavy tree cover may need more frequent service.
