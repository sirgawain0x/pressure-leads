## Why Commercial Properties Need Regular Pressure Washing

### Safety and Liability
Algae, mold, and grease on walkways and parking areas create slip-and-fall hazards. In Florida, property owners can be held liable for injuries caused by negligent maintenance. Regular pressure washing demonstrates proactive property care and reduces your liability exposure.

### Code Compliance
Many Northeast Florida municipalities have exterior property maintenance codes. St. Johns County and the City of St. Augustine both require commercial properties to maintain clean, well-kept exteriors. Regular cleaning helps you stay in compliance and avoid fines.

### Customer Perception
Research consistently shows that clean business exteriors increase foot traffic and customer confidence. A well-maintained property signals professionalism, attention to detail, and pride in your business.

### Tenant Retention
For property managers and HOAs, clean common areas and building exteriors contribute directly to tenant satisfaction and retention. Residents and commercial tenants expect maintained properties.

### Asset Protection
Florida's climate accelerates building deterioration. Mold, algae, and pollution deposits break down paint, stucco, and concrete over time. Regular cleaning extends the life of exterior surfaces and reduces long-term maintenance costs.
