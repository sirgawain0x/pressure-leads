## Why You Should Never Pressure Wash a Roof

This is critical: **high-pressure washing will destroy your roof.** Many well-meaning homeowners or inexperienced contractors make this mistake every year, causing:

- **Granule loss on asphalt shingles** — Pressure strips the protective granule layer, dramatically shortening roof life
- **Cracked and broken tiles** — Concrete and clay tiles can crack under high pressure
- **Water intrusion** — Pressure forces water under shingles and flashing, causing leaks and wood rot
- **Voided manufacturer warranty** — Most shingle manufacturers explicitly prohibit high-pressure cleaning

That's why we exclusively use the **soft wash method** for all roof cleaning. Soft washing applies a specialized cleaning solution at low pressure (under 100 PSI) that kills algae, mold, and moss at the root. The organisms die and the stains wash away naturally with rainfall over the following days.

## What's Growing on Your Roof?

Florida roofs commonly develop several types of organic growth:

- **Gloeocapsa magma (black streaks)** — The most common roof stain in Florida. Appears as dark streaks running vertically down the roof. Feeds on limestone in shingles.
- **Green algae** — Bright green patches that thrive in shaded, moist areas of the roof
- **Moss** — Thick, green growth that lifts shingles and traps moisture underneath
- **Lichen** — A combination of algae and fungus that bonds aggressively to roofing material
- **Mold and mildew** — Dark spots that can spread to attic spaces and affect indoor air quality

All of these organisms hold moisture against your roofing material, accelerating deterioration and potentially leading to leaks and structural damage.

Most roofs show dramatic improvement immediately after treatment. However, some deep stains (especially heavy Gloeocapsa magma) continue to fade over 1–4 weeks as dead algae washes off with rain.

### Is the cleaning solution safe for my gutters and downspouts?
Yes. Our solution rinses cleanly through gutters and downspouts without causing corrosion or staining. We also rinse all runoff from landscaping areas.

### Can you clean my roof in the rainy season?
Yes. Our cleaning solution bonds to roofing surfaces and remains effective even if it rains shortly after application. In fact, rain helps wash away dead algae faster.

### How long does a roof soft wash last?
Results typically last 2–3 years in Northeast Florida. Homes with heavy tree coverage may see regrowth sooner, while roofs with full sun exposure stay cleaner longer.
